Bill Harris is an accomplished artist known for bold color, striking light, and expressive subjects in oil. His work has been featured in galleries throughout the region and continues to evolve with creativity and depth. To register or ask questions, contact by text or email below.

Classes are offered on Tuesdays (6–8pm), Fridays (10am–12 noon), and Sundays (11am–1pm or 2–4pm), running in 5-week sessions. You'll learn Bill Harris’ oil painting techniques and complete a full painting over four classes. No experience needed — all are welcome!
